Catfish Pepper Soup

Ingredients: Fresh catfish, uziza leaves, pepper, seasoning cubes, onions, ginger, garlic, scent leaves

Instructions: Clean catfish, season with spices and boil with pepper and onions. Add scent leaves at the end for aroma.

Fresh catfish gives the best authentic taste ‚Äî avoid frozen for pepper soup.

Grilled Tilapia with Pepper Sauce

Ingredients: Whole tilapia, vegetable oil, pepper mix, garlic, ginger, lime juice, seasoning

Instructions: Marinate fish in pepper mix, grill until golden. Serve with fried yam or plantain.

Wrap fish in foil for smoky ‚Äúsuya-style‚Äù grilling.

Nigerian Fish Stew

Ingredients: Fresh croaker/titus fish, tomatoes, bell pepper, onions, palm oil, seasoning cubes

Instructions: Fry blended tomato-pepper mix, add spices, then gently add fried or grilled fish. Simmer.

Use titus (mackerel) for rich taste and affordability.

Egg Sauce & Boiled Yam

Ingredients: Eggs, tomatoes, onions, peppers, vegetable oil, salt, seasoning

Instructions: Fry tomato-pepper-onion mix, pour in beaten eggs, scramble lightly. Serve with boiled yam.

Add sardines or corned beef to make it richer.

Akara with Egg

Ingredients: Peeled beans, pepper, onions, palm oil/veg oil, eggs

Instructions: Blend beans with pepper & onions, fry as akara balls, serve with fried or boiled eggs.

Enjoy with pap (ogi) for a traditional Nigerian breakfast.

Chicken Suya

Ingredients: Chicken breast/thigh, suya spice, groundnut oil, onions, cabbage

Instructions: Coat chicken with suya spice, grill until tender. Serve with onions and cabbage.

Wrap in newspaper for authentic street suya vibes!

Nigerian Chicken Stew

Ingredients: Chicken, blended tomatoes, peppers, onions, curry, thyme, vegetable oil

Instructions: Parboil chicken with spices, fry tomato-pepper mix, then add chicken and broth. Simmer.

Perfect with rice, yam, or spaghetti.

Party Jollof Rice with Chicken

Ingredients: Long-grain rice, chicken, tomato-pepper mix, onions, spices, chicken stock

Instructions: Fry pepper mix with oil, add rice and chicken stock, cook until smoky. Serve with fried/grilled chicken.

For authentic smoky taste, finish cooking on low fire.

Chicken Pepper Soup

Ingredients: Chicken parts, uziza seeds, ginger, garlic, pepper, onions, scent leaves

Instructions: Cook chicken with spices, add ground uziza and scent leaves. Simmer until fragrant.

Great for cold nights or recovery meals.
